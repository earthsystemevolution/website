Earth System Evolution group

The Earth System Evolution group is an interdisciplinary group based at the Department of Earth Science at the University of Bergen (UiB) in Norway. We are part of the K.G. Jebsen Centre for Deep Sea Research) and the Bjerknes Centre for Climate Research (BCCR). Formerly, we were part of the Centre for Geobiology (CGB), which was superseeded by the K.G. Jebsen Centre for Deep Sea Research in May 2017.

We’re scientists with backgrounds in geology, theoretical physics, paleoclimatology, paleontology, microbiology, and geomicrobiology and geochemistry. Currently, the lineup is:

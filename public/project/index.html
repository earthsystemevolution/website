Projects

Our research revolves around three main topics.

  1. 1. Causal interactions in deep time

    Quantifying dynamical interactions in the Earth System across time scales.

  2. 2. Dynamical system invariants and information flow

    Establishing connections between probability-based and dynamical systems-based causality measures.

  3. 3. Energy landscapes and microbial interactions in hydrothermal systems

    Modeling the influence of energy availability on microbial interactions in hydrothermal systems
